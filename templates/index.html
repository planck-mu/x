ü™ê The Enigma of Black Holes

Black holes are regions in space where gravity is so intense that not even light can escape. At their core lies the **singularity**, a point of infinite density. The study of black holes is reshaping our understanding of gravity, space, and time. Recent discoveries, including the **Event Horizon Telescope's image** of a black hole in the galaxy M87, are providing new insights into the nature of these cosmic giants.

üåü Quantum Mechanics: The Bizarre Nature of Reality

Quantum mechanics describes the behavior of particles on the smallest scales. At this scale, particles exist in multiple states at once (superposition) and can be **entangled**, meaning the state of one particle instantaneously affects the state of another, even at vast distances. These bizarre phenomena challenge our classical understanding of reality and form the basis of technologies such as **quantum computing** and **quantum encryption**.
